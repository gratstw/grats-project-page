GRATS Dataset Terms of Use Draft

This is a draft. Please confirm with the lab/institution before publication.

1. The dataset is provided for non-commercial academic research use only unless separate written permission is granted.
2. Users may not redistribute the raw audio, video, metadata, or any speaker-identifiable derived data.
3. Users may not attempt to identify speakers or recover personal information.
4. Users must cite the corresponding GRATS or AV-GRATS paper when using the dataset or benchmark protocol.
5. Users must follow all additional conditions in the dataset access agreement.
6. Access may be revoked if the terms are violated.
